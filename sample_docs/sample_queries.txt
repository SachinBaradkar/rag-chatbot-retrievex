# Sample Queries for RAG Chatbot Testing

## After uploading Employee_Handbook.md

Basic retrieval:
- How many days of annual leave do employees get?
- What is the sick leave policy?
- What is the notice period for resignation?

Multi-turn conversation:
- What is the maternity leave policy?
  → How does that compare to paternity leave?
  → Is it fully paid?

Grievance and conduct:
- Explain the grievance procedure step by step.
- What happens if I have a conflict of interest?
- What constitutes gross misconduct?

Remote work:
- Can I work from home every day?
- What are the core hours for remote workers?

Performance:
- How often are performance reviews held?
- What happens if I get a "Needs Improvement" rating twice?

Compensation:
- What percentage of health insurance does the company cover?
- How much does the company contribute to provident fund?
